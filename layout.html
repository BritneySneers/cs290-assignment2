Radio Button Testcase: Candy





The League used to be on FX, but recently switched to FXX, where its ratings dropped somewhat precipitously.

The League is an FXX show featuring the snarky and mean-spirited antics of a handful of Chicagoans who play together in a fantasy football league. While those with football knowledge will get more of the show's witty in-jokes, no knowledge of football is truly necessary to appreciate the well written humor evident throughout the program. (Indeed, I detest spectator sports and haven't watched a football game aside from the Superbowl since 1997)

The League also has its share of unhinged peripheral characters. Most notable among these is Rafi, the depraved, borderline-insane individual with no sense of social norms or personal boundaries. His best friend, Dirty Randy, played by Seth Rogan in a recurring capacity, also displays a remarkable lack of tact and diplomacy, not to mention an overall dissociation with reality.
